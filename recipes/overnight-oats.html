Overnight Oats

Description

This is a make-ahead breaf=kfast that will keep your stomach full until lunchtime

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons chia seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon gorund cinnamon
  • 1/4 fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Combine milk, yoghurt, oats, honey, chia seeds and cinnamon in a 1/2 pint jar with alid and shake until combined.
  3. Fold in blueberries
  4. Cover an refigerate 8 hours overnight. Enjoy!
