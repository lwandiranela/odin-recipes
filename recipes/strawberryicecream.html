Strawberry Ice-Cream

Description

Make a delicious, rich, creamy strawberry ice cream dull of fresh and fruity flavour

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 drops red food colouring

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food colouring in a large bowl
  3. Pour strawberry mixture intoo the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the maufcaturer's directions.
  4. Transfer into an airtight container and freeze until firm
