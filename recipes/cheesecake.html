Vegan Mars Bars Cheesecake

Description

Make a chocolate caramel cheesecake that nobody would know is vegan! Everyone will simply adore this sweet anf healthier version of cheesecake. I made this for my family over this Christmas holidays and the whole family simply could not get enough of it

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cashews
  • 1 cup cream of coconut
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup almonds
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 4 ounces dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Soak cashews enough in water and cover for 2 to 3 hours.
  2. Bring coconut cream, brown sugar, and salt for caramel sauce to a boil in a saucepan, reduce heat and let simmer until slightly thick, for 5 minutes. Transfer to the fridge, it will get thivker as it cools.
  3. Blend almonds and coconut oil together in a bowl of a food processor or an electric blender. Scoop out and pat down into a springorm pan. Place into the freezer
  4. Blend 2/3 cup caramel sauce and soaked cashews together in a blender for the caramel filling, pour on top of the cake base. Place back into the freezer.
  5. Place dark chocolate for the chocolate filling in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Stir frequently, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula to avoid scorching, until chocolate is melted, for about 5 minutes. Pour onto cake and return to the freezer.
  6. Place cake back into the freezer until frozen, for about 2 hours. Remove the cake and let it defrost for about 10 minutes before cutting
